01 — About

Where operations meets engineering

I started on the shop floor — folding garments at Zara and H&M, scanning barcodes at Aeropostale, managing shifts, handling cash, and listening to customers. Nine years later, I run collections operations at eNovate and lead Axentro, an AI-assisted product studio building the tools I once wished I had.

My edge is simple: I build the systems I operate. Aging reports, invoice trackers, attendance logs, merchant pipelines — I've lived inside these workflows, found the friction, then engineered software to remove it. That means every product I ship is grounded in real operational pain, not theoretical dashboards.

At eNovate, I lead accounts receivable and collections strategy — chasing overdue invoices, negotiating payment plans, and keeping the cash flow healthy. At Axentro, I design and ship AI-assisted business systems using GitHub, Supabase, Google Apps Script, and modern web technology.

I don't separate the business mind from the builder mind. They are the same mind — and that's the unfair advantage I bring to every project, client, and team.
